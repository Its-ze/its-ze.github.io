About Me

Get to know me, my journey, and what drives my passion for technology, creativity, and sharing knowledge.

About Us

My name is Z. My pronouns are They/Them, and I'm currently 20 years old. I hold an Associate of Arts and an Associate of Science in Cyber and Information Security from Lorain County Community College (LCCC).

I've been using Linux for around 4 years, and I've been working in the IT field professionally for about 2 years. Linux has been a major part of my personal and professional life, and I enjoy learning new things about technology, security, and open-source software.

My partner, S, is also a part of this project and will be contributing short stories alongside me. Together, we aim to share our creative endeavors, whether it be blog posts, projects, or imaginative stories.

My Interests and Hobbies

When I'm not working or experimenting with technology, I enjoy a variety of hobbies that help me stay balanced and creative. Here are a few things that keep me busy:

  • Creative Writing: Besides my work in tech, I love creative writing. My partner and I often collaborate on short stories that explore different genres, from science fiction to fantasy.
  • 3D Printing: I enjoy tinkering with 3D printers, creating both practical items and fun models. It’s amazing to see ideas come to life in a tangible form.
  • Landscaping: My first job was in landscaping, and it still holds a special place in my heart. Whenever I can, I spend time in my garden or help friends and family with their outdoor projects.
  • Gaming: Video games are another way I like to relax. They give me a chance to explore new worlds, solve problems, and have a little fun in between work sessions.

My Professional Journey

My journey into IT started at a young age, driven by a fascination with how things work behind the scenes. I decided to formalize my passion with studies in Cyber and Information Security at LCCC. Since then, I've been fortunate enough to work professionally in IT, gaining hands-on experience with a range of technologies and systems.

Currently, I am expanding my skill set by diving deeper into cybersecurity, Linux administration, and cloud solutions. I firmly believe in lifelong learning, and I'm excited to continue growing in this ever-changing industry.

Why This Website?

This website is a place for me to document my journey, share my thoughts, and connect with others who share similar passions. Whether it's a new blog post on technology, a project I've been working on, or a collaborative short story with my partner, I hope to create a space that is both informative and inspiring for anyone visiting.
