Introduction

As my side IT business and full-time job both continue to expand, managing my workload efficiently has become crucial. I've tried different task management systems, and I recently started using Any.do to streamline everything I need to keep track of—from client maintenance tasks to my personal projects. Here’s how Any.do has helped me take control of my time.

Why Any.do?

After exploring several options, I chose Any.do for a few reasons. One of the most significant is its seamless integration with both my Google Calendar and Microsoft 365. Having one place where I can add, track, and update tasks that synchronize across my professional and personal calendars has been a game-changer.

The platform is primarily a web-based app, which means I can access it on any of my devices, no matter which operating system I’m using. I use Linux for most of my work, and Any.do being platform-agnostic means I’m not stuck looking for compatibility solutions. Furthermore, Any.do's natural language processing (NLP) capabilities make adding tasks incredibly simple. Instead of manually inputting due dates or reminders, I can just type something like, “Finish website redesign by Friday,” and Any.do knows exactly what to do.

Breaking Down My Workflow
